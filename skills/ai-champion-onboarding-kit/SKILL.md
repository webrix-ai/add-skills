# AI Champion Onboarding Kit

Generate an onboarding kit for department AI champions including training materials, success metrics, and rollout playbook.

**Integrations:** Google Drive, Notion

## When to Use

- The user is setting up an AI champions program
- A team needs to identify and onboard AI advocates within departments
- The user mentions "AI champion", "AI advocate", "train the trainer", or "AI rollout"
- Organizations want a grassroots approach to AI adoption

## Steps

### Step 1: Define the Champion Role

Help the user define the AI Champion responsibilities:

| Responsibility | Description |
|---------------|-------------|
| **Advocate** | Promote AI tool usage and share success stories within their team |
| **Trainer** | Run hands-on workshops and pair-programming sessions with AI tools |
| **Support** | Be the first point of contact for AI tool questions in their department |
| **Feedback** | Collect feedback from their team and relay to the central AI team |
| **Compliance** | Ensure their team follows the AI usage policy |
| **Metrics** | Track and report adoption metrics for their department |

### Step 2: Create Training Materials

Generate a training curriculum:

**Module 1: AI Fundamentals (30 min)**
- How LLMs work (high-level, non-technical)
- Prompt engineering basics
- Understanding AI limitations and hallucinations
- Data privacy considerations

**Module 2: Tool-Specific Training (60 min per tool)**
- Setup and configuration walkthrough
- Top 10 workflows for each approved tool
- Tips and tricks from power users
- Common pitfalls to avoid

**Module 3: Leading AI Adoption (45 min)**
- Change management principles
- Handling resistance and skepticism
- Measuring and communicating success
- Escalation paths for issues

**Module 4: Security & Compliance (30 min)**
- AI usage policy overview
- Data classification quick reference
- What to do when things go wrong
- Audit and reporting responsibilities

### Step 3: Build the Rollout Playbook

Create a week-by-week rollout plan:

| Week | Activity | Champion Action |
|------|----------|----------------|
| 1 | Champion Training | Complete all training modules, get certified |
| 2 | Team Assessment | Survey team on current AI usage and interest |
| 3 | Kickoff Workshop | Run first team workshop on primary AI tool |
| 4 | Pair Programming | Schedule 1:1 sessions with interested team members |
| 5-6 | Open Office Hours | Weekly drop-in sessions for questions |
| 7-8 | Measure & Report | Collect first metrics, share wins |
| 9-12 | Sustain & Scale | Monthly workshops, advanced topics, peer sharing |

### Step 4: Define Success Metrics

| Metric | Target (by 90 days) | How to Measure |
|--------|---------------------|----------------|
| Adoption Rate | 70% of team actively using AI tools | Tool login data / SSO |
| Training Completion | 100% of team completes Module 1 | LMS or sign-off sheet |
| Satisfaction Score | 4+ out of 5 | Monthly pulse survey |
| Time Saved | 5+ hours/week per developer | Self-reported survey |
| Support Tickets | <3 escalations per month | Jira/Slack tracking |

### Step 5: Generate Deliverables

Create in Google Drive or Notion:

1. **Champion Welcome Packet** — Role description, expectations, time commitment, support resources
2. **Training Slide Decks** — One per module, ready to present
3. **Workshop Templates** — Hands-on exercises for team workshops
4. **Survey Templates** — Pre-adoption baseline and monthly pulse surveys
5. **Metrics Dashboard Template** — Spreadsheet for tracking department-level metrics
6. **Communication Templates** — Email templates for team announcements, success stories, tips of the week

## Output

Deliver:
- Complete onboarding packet (Google Drive folder or Notion workspace)
- Training curriculum with slide templates
- Rollout playbook with timeline
- Metrics tracking spreadsheet
- Communication templates
